Welcome to Savanna's Coffee House where we roast our own coffee from only the best coffee sources around the world. Our prices are low and we offer a range of beverages, sandwiches, salads, and homemade soups; along with a wide variety of cakes, pastries, and desserts. See our Menu for more.

We offer a strong, fast, and free Wifi connection, providing a comfy workspace for the average college student, as well as an incredible lounging area to enjoy company with your friends and fellow peers. Local entertainment is also provided on weekend evenings and you can find those events on the Events tab.
